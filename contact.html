Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

How quickly can you start managing our campaigns?

+

We can typically start managing your campaigns within 3-5 business days after our initial consultation and contract signing. This includes pixel setup, campaign creation, and initial optimization.

Do you require long-term contracts?

+

No, we don't require long-term contracts. We work on a month-to-month basis because we're confident in our ability to deliver results that will make you want to continue working with us.

What platforms do you manage campaigns on?

+

We manage campaigns across all major platforms including Facebook, Instagram, Google Ads, YouTube, LinkedIn, and TikTok. We'll recommend the best platforms based on your target audience and business goals.

How do you measure and report campaign performance?

+

We provide detailed weekly and monthly reports covering key metrics like ROAS, CPA, conversion rates, and more. You'll also have access to a real-time dashboard to monitor performance 24/7.

What's included in your pixel setup service?

+

Our pixel setup includes Facebook Pixel, Google Analytics 4, Google Tag Manager configuration, conversion tracking, custom event setup, and cross-platform attribution to ensure accurate measurement.
