Capstone Project Overview

Capstone Project হলো comprehensive digital marketing campaign যা সব শেখা skills integrate করে। এটি portfolio এর centerpiece হবে।

Project এর উদ্দেশ্য
  • Skill Integration: সব modules এর knowledge combine
  • Real-world Application: Practical experience gain
  • Portfolio Development: Professional showcase creation
  • Problem Solving: Business challenges address
  • Results Demonstration: Measurable outcomes achieve

Project Planning Framework

Successful project execution এর জন্য systematic planning approach জরুরি।

Project Planning Phases:

  • Discovery Phase: Business analysis ও goal setting
  • Strategy Phase: Marketing strategy development
  • Planning Phase: Tactical planning ও timeline
  • Execution Phase: Campaign implementation
  • Monitoring Phase: Performance tracking
  • Optimization Phase: Continuous improvement
  • Reporting Phase: Results analysis ও presentation

Project Charter Template:

  • Project Title: Clear, descriptive name
  • Business Background: Company/client overview
  • Problem Statement: Challenge identification
  • Project Objectives: SMART goals
  • Success Metrics: KPIs ও measurement
  • Target Audience: Detailed personas
  • Budget Allocation: Resource distribution
  • Timeline: Milestones ও deadlines
  • Deliverables: Expected outcomes
Project Selection Tips

Real business choose করুন যেখানে actual impact create করতে পারবেন। Permission নিয়ে work করুন।

Business Analysis ও Research

Thorough business analysis project success এর foundation।

Business Analysis Components:

  • Company Overview: History, mission, values
  • Industry Analysis: Market size, trends, growth
  • Competitive Analysis: Direct ও indirect competitors
  • SWOT Analysis: Internal ও external factors
  • Customer Analysis: Demographics, psychographics
  • Current Marketing: Existing efforts evaluation
  • Digital Presence: Website, social media audit

Research Methodologies:

  • Primary Research: Surveys, interviews, observations
  • Secondary Research: Industry reports, competitor analysis
  • Digital Analytics: Website, social media data
  • Market Research: Trend analysis, consumer behavior
  • Competitive Intelligence: Competitor strategies

Key Research Questions:

  • What are the business objectives?
  • Who is the target audience?
  • What's the current market position?
  • What are the main challenges?
  • What resources are available?
  • What's the competitive landscape?
  • What are customer pain points?

Campaign Themes ও Messaging:

  • Call-to-Actions: Conversion drivers
  • Brand Voice: Communication style

Performance Measurement

KPI Framework:

Awareness Metrics:
  • Reach: Total audience exposed
  • Impressions: Content views
  • Brand Mentions: Social media mentions
  • Share of Voice: Competitive comparison
  • Website Traffic: Organic ও paid visits
Engagement Metrics:
  • Click-through Rate: Ad ও content engagement
  • Social Engagement: Likes, comments, shares
  • Time on Site: Content consumption
  • Page Views: Website exploration
  • Email Open Rate: Message engagement
Conversion Metrics:
  • Conversion Rate: Goal completion percentage
  • Cost per Acquisition: Customer acquisition cost
  • Return on Ad Spend: Advertising ROI
  • Lead Quality: Qualification metrics
  • Customer Lifetime Value: Long-term value

Reporting Dashboard Setup:

  • Google Analytics: Website performance
  • Google Data Studio: Unified reporting
  • Social Media Analytics: Platform insights
  • Email Analytics: Campaign performance
  • Advertising Dashboards: Paid campaign metrics
