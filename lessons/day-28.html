Privacy ও Data Protection

Privacy-first marketing এখন necessity। GDPR, iOS 14.5+ changes marketing landscape পরিবর্তন করেছে।

Privacy Regulations Impact:

  • GDPR (Europe): Consent-based data collection
  • CCPA (California): Consumer privacy rights
  • iOS 14.5+: App tracking transparency
  • Chrome Cookieless: Third-party cookie phase-out
  • Bangladesh Data Protection: Emerging regulations

Cookieless Marketing Strategies:

  • First-party Data: Direct customer data collection
  • Zero-party Data: Voluntarily shared information
  • Contextual Advertising: Content-based targeting
  • Server-side Tracking: Enhanced measurement
  • Customer Data Platforms: Unified data management

Privacy-Compliant Tactics:

  • Transparent Communication: Clear privacy policies
  • Value Exchange: Benefits for data sharing
  • Progressive Profiling: Gradual data collection
  • Consent Management: User preference centers
