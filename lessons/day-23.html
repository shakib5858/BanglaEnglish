Social Media ROI

Social Media ROI calculate করা challenging কিন্তু business success এর জন্য জরুরি।

ROI Calculation Methods:

  • Direct Sales: Social থেকে direct revenue
  • Lead Generation: Qualified leads value
  • Customer Acquisition: New customer cost
  • Brand Value: Awareness ও sentiment impact
  • Customer Service: Support cost savings

ROI Formula:

Social Media ROI Calculation:

ROI = (Social Media Revenue - Social Media Investment) / Social Media Investment × 100

Example:
Revenue from Social Media: ৫০,০০০ টাকা
Social Media Investment: ২০,০০০ টাকা
ROI = (৫০,০০০ - ২০,০০০) / ২০,০০০ × ১০০ = ১৫০%

Investment includes:
- Ad spend
- Tool costs
- Staff time
- Content creation costs

Value Attribution:

  • First-Touch: First social interaction credit
  • Last-Touch: Final social touchpoint credit
  • Multi-Touch: Distributed credit across touchpoints
  • Assisted Conversions: Social media influence tracking

Key Metrics Tracking

Essential KPIs by Platform:

Facebook KPIs:
  • Reach: Unique users who saw content
  • Engagement Rate: (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Reach
  • Click-Through Rate: Clicks / Impressions
  • Cost Per Click: Ad spend / Clicks
  • Video Completion Rate: Full video views / Total views
Instagram KPIs:
  • Engagement Rate: Total engagement / Followers
  • Story Completion Rate: Story views to end
  • Profile Visits: Profile page views
  • Website Clicks: Bio link clicks
  • Hashtag Performance: Reach from hashtags
LinkedIn KPIs:
  • Follower Growth Rate: New followers / Total followers
  • Engagement Rate: Reactions + Comments + Shares / Impressions
  • Click-Through Rate: Clicks / Impressions
  • Lead Generation: Form fills from LinkedIn
  • Employee Advocacy: Employee sharing rate
