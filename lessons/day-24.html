Attribution Models

Attribution Models বিভিন্ন touchpoints এর মধ্যে conversion credit distribute করে।

Standard Attribution Models:

  • Last Click: Final touchpoint gets 100% credit
  • First Click: Initial touchpoint gets 100% credit
  • Linear: Equal credit across all touchpoints
  • Time Decay: Recent touchpoints get more credit
  • Position-based: First ও last get 40% each, middle gets 20%
  • Data-driven: AI-based credit distribution

Attribution Model Comparison:

Customer Journey Example:
Facebook Ad → Google Search → Email → Direct → Purchase

Last Click: Direct gets 100% credit
First Click: Facebook gets 100% credit
Linear: Each gets 25% credit
Time Decay: Direct 40%, Email 30%, Google 20%, Facebook 10%
Position-based: Facebook 40%, Direct 40%, Google+Email 20%

Model Selection Criteria:

  • Business Type: B2B vs B2C journey length
  • Sales Cycle: Short vs long consideration period
  • Channel Mix: Number of marketing channels
  • Data Availability: Historical conversion data
