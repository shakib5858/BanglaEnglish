Email List Building

Email List হলো email marketing এর foundation। Quality list ছাড়া successful email marketing সম্ভব নয়।

List Building Strategies:

  • Lead Magnets: Free resources offer
  • Newsletter Signup: Website এ subscription form
  • Content Upgrades: Blog post related bonuses
  • Social Media: Platform integration
  • Events: Webinars, workshops
  • Partnerships: Cross-promotion

Lead Magnet Ideas:

  • eBooks: Comprehensive guides
  • Checklists: Step-by-step lists
  • Templates: Ready-to-use resources
  • Webinars: Educational sessions
  • Free Trials: Product sampling
  • Discounts: Special offers
List Building Don'ts

কখনো email list কিনবেন না বা spam করবেন না। Permission-based marketing করুন।

Email Campaign Types

বিভিন্ন ধরনের email campaign বিভিন্ন উদ্দেশ্যে ব্যবহার করা হয়।

Campaign Categories:

  • Welcome Series: New subscriber onboarding
  • Newsletter: Regular content updates
  • Promotional: Sales ও offers
  • Educational: Tips ও tutorials
  • Transactional: Order confirmations, receipts
  • Re-engagement: Inactive subscriber activation

Email Frequency Guidelines:

  • Welcome Series: 3-5 emails over 1 week
  • Newsletter: Weekly বা bi-weekly
  • Promotional: Monthly বা seasonal
  • Educational: Weekly
  • Re-engagement: Quarterly

Email Segmentation

Email Segmentation করলে open rate ৩৯% এবং click rate ২৮% বৃদ্ধি পায়।

Segmentation Criteria:

  • Demographics: Age, gender, location
  • Behavior: Purchase history, website activity
  • Preferences: Content interests, frequency
  • Engagement: Active, inactive subscribers
  • Customer Journey: New, existing, churned

Segmentation Examples:

  • New Subscribers: Welcome series
  • Frequent Buyers: VIP offers
  • Cart Abandoners: Reminder emails
  • Location-based: Local events, offers
  • Interest-based: Relevant content

Email Design ও Content

Email Template Structure:

Essential Elements:
  • Header: Logo ও branding
  • Subject Line: Compelling headline
  • Preheader: Preview text
  • Body Content: Main message
  • Call-to-Action: Clear button/link
  • Footer: Contact info ও unsubscribe
Design Best Practices:
  • Mobile-First: ৫০%+ emails mobile এ খোলা হয়
  • Single Column: Mobile-friendly layout
  • Clear Hierarchy: Scannable content
  • Brand Consistency: Colors, fonts, style
  • Image Optimization: Fast loading

Subject Line Best Practices:

  • Length: 30-50 characters optimal
  • Personalization: Name বা location use
  • Urgency: Time-sensitive offers
  • Curiosity: Questions বা teasers
  • A/B Testing: Different versions test

Email Automation

Automation Workflows:

Welcome Series (5-email sequence):
  1. Email 1: Welcome ও thank you
  2. Email 2: Brand story ও values
  3. Email 3: Best content/products
  4. Email 4: Social proof ও testimonials
  5. Email 5: Special offer বা next steps
Abandoned Cart Series:
  1. 1 hour later: Gentle reminder
  2. 24 hours later: Product benefits
  3. 3 days later: Discount offer

Automation Triggers:

  • Signup: Welcome series start
  • Purchase: Thank you ও upsell
  • Birthday: Special offers
  • Inactivity: Re-engagement campaign
  • Website Behavior: Targeted content
