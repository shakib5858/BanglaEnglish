SEO Content Writing

SEO Content লেখার সময় search engines ও readers দুটোকেই মাথায় রাখতে হবে।

SEO Content Elements:

  • Keyword Research: Target keywords identify
  • Title Optimization: Compelling ও keyword-rich titles
  • Meta Description: Search snippet optimization
  • Header Tags: H1, H2, H3 structure
  • Internal Linking: Related content connection
  • Image Optimization: Alt text ও file names

Content Structure Framework:

SEO Blog Post Structure:

1. Compelling Title (H1)
   - Include primary keyword
   - 50-60 characters optimal

2. Introduction (150-200 words)
   - Hook to grab attention
   - Promise what readers will learn
   - Include primary keyword

3. Main Content (1500-2500 words)
   - Use H2, H3 subheadings
   - Include secondary keywords
   - Add bullet points ও lists
   - Use short paragraphs

4. Conclusion
   - Summarize key points
   - Include call-to-action
   - Encourage engagement

5. Meta Elements
   - Meta description (150-160 chars)
   - Alt text for images
   - Internal ও external links

Website Copywriting

Website Copy visitors কে customers এ convert করার জন্য persuasive ও clear হতে হবে।

Key Website Pages:

  • Homepage: First impression ও value proposition
  • About Page: Brand story ও credibility
  • Service/Product Pages: Features ও benefits
  • Contact Page: Easy communication
  • Landing Pages: Conversion-focused

Copywriting Principles:

  • Clear Value Proposition: What you offer
  • Benefits over Features: Customer-focused
  • Social Proof: Testimonials ও reviews
  • Strong CTAs: Clear next steps
  • Scannable Format: Easy to read

Content Writing Best Practices

Quality content তৈরির জন্য proven techniques follow করুন।

Writing Guidelines:

  • Know Your Audience: Reader persona বুঝুন
  • Simple Language: Jargon avoid করুন
  • Active Voice: Passive voice এর চেয়ে engaging
  • Short Sentences: Readability improve করে
  • Storytelling: Narrative elements add করুন

Content Optimization:

  • Readability Score: Grade 8-10 level target
  • Sentence Length: 15-20 words average
  • Paragraph Length: 2-3 sentences max
  • Transition Words: Flow improve করে
  • Visual Elements: Images, videos, infographics
