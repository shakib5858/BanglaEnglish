Advanced Email Automation

Email Automation ব্যবহার করে ৩২০% বেশি revenue generate করা যায়। এটি right message, right time এ right person এর কাছে পৌঁছায়।

Automation এর সুবিধা
  • Time Saving: Manual work কমায়
  • Personalization: Individual behavior based
  • Consistency: Regular communication maintain
  • Scalability: Large audience handle
  • Higher Conversion: Relevant messaging

Advanced Automation Types:

  • Behavioral Triggers: User actions based
  • Date-based: Birthdays, anniversaries
  • Lifecycle Emails: Customer journey stages
  • Re-engagement: Inactive subscriber activation
  • Cross-sell/Upsell: Product recommendations
  • Win-back: Churned customer recovery

Behavioral Email Marketing

Behavioral Emails user এর specific actions এর উপর ভিত্তি করে পাঠানো হয়। এগুলো ৫০% বেশি open rate পায়।

Behavioral Triggers:

  • Website Visit: Specific page visit
  • Product View: Product page browsing
  • Cart Abandonment: Items left in cart
  • Purchase: Transaction completion
  • Download: Content download
  • Email Engagement: Click, open patterns

Behavioral Email Examples:

Trigger Email Type Send Time Purpose

Cart Abandon Reminder 1 hour later Recovery
Purchase Thank You Immediately Confirmation
