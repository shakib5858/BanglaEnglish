Video Production Basics

Quality video production এর জন্য proper planning ও execution প্রয়োজন।

Pre-Production:

  • Script Writing: Clear message ও structure
  • Storyboard: Visual planning
  • Location: Appropriate setting
  • Equipment: Camera, lighting, audio
  • Talent: Presenter বা actors

Production:

  • Lighting: Natural বা artificial lighting
  • Audio: Clear sound recording
  • Camera Work: Stable shots, good framing
  • Multiple Takes: Options for editing

Post-Production:

  • Editing: Cutting, transitions, effects
  • Color Grading: Visual enhancement
  • Audio Mixing: Sound optimization
  • Graphics: Text, logos, animations

Video Production Setup

Equipment Requirements:

Budget Setup (৫,০০০-১৫,০০০ টাকা):
  • Camera: Smartphone with good camera
  • Tripod: Mobile tripod (৫০০-১০০০ টাকা)
  • Lighting: Ring light (১০০০-২০০০ টাকা)
  • Audio: Lapel microphone (৫০০-১৫০০ টাকা)
  • Background: Simple backdrop
Professional Setup (২০,০০০-৫০,০০০ টাকা):
  • Camera: DSLR/Mirrorless camera
  • Lenses: 50mm portrait lens
  • Lighting Kit: 3-point lighting setup
  • Audio: Wireless microphone system
  • Editing: Professional editing software

Video Editing Tools

Mobile Editing Apps:

  • CapCut: Free, user-friendly
  • InShot: Quick editing
  • Adobe Premiere Rush: Professional features
  • Canva Video: Template-based

Desktop Editing Software:

  • DaVinci Resolve: Free, professional
  • Adobe Premiere Pro: Industry standard
  • Final Cut Pro: Mac users
  • Filmora: Beginner-friendly

Basic Editing Workflow:

  1. Import footage ও organize
  2. Rough cut তৈরি করুন
  3. Audio sync ও cleanup
  4. Color correction
  5. Graphics ও text add
  6. Final export
