Effective Prompt Engineering

Prompt Engineering AI থেকে quality output পাওয়ার key।

Good Prompt Elements
  • Clear Context: Background information provide
  • Specific Instructions: Exact requirements
  • Target Audience: Who will read/see
  • Tone & Style: Brand voice specification
  • Format Requirements: Length, structure

Prompt Structure Framework:

Effective Prompt Template:

Role: "Act as a [specific role]"
Context: "For a [type of business/audience]"
Task: "Create [specific content type]"
Requirements: "Include [specific elements]"
Tone: "Use [tone/style]"
Format: "[length/structure requirements]"

Example:
"Act as a digital marketing expert for a small restaurant in Dhaka. Create a Facebook post about our new Bengali fusion menu. Include a call-to-action, use friendly conversational tone, and keep it under 150 words with relevant hashtags."

ChatGPT Mastery

ChatGPT Setup ও Usage:

  1. Account Creation:
    chat.openai.com → Sign up → Choose plan
  2. Interface Understanding:
    Chat interface, model selection, conversation history
  3. Basic Prompting:
    Simple content requests practice
  4. Advanced Techniques:
    Chain prompting, role-playing, iterative refinement

ChatGPT Content Creation Examples:

Blog Post Prompt:
"Write a 800-word blog post about 'Digital Marketing Tips for Small Businesses in Bangladesh'. Include an introduction, 5 main tips with explanations, and a conclusion with call-to-action. Use conversational tone and include relevant Bengali business examples."

Social Media Prompt:
"Create 5 Facebook posts for a local bakery in Dhaka. Each post should promote different products (cake, pastry, bread, cookies, custom orders). Include engaging captions, relevant hashtags, and call-to-actions. Keep each post under 100 words."

Email Subject Lines:
"Generate 10 email subject lines for a digital marketing course enrollment campaign. Target audience is small business owners in Bangladesh. Focus on benefits, urgency, and curiosity. Include both English and Bengali options."
