TikTok Algorithm বোঝা

TikTok এর For You Page (FYP) algorithm বুঝলে content viral করা সহজ।

Algorithm Factors
  • Video Information: Captions, sounds, hashtags
  • User Interactions: Likes, shares, comments, follows
  • Device Settings: Language, location, device type
  • Completion Rate: Video কতটুকু দেখা হলো
  • Re-watch Rate: Video কতবার replay হলো

Algorithm Optimization Tips:

  • Hook in First 3 Seconds: Attention grab করুন
  • Keep Viewers Till End: Completion rate বাড়ান
  • Encourage Engagement: Comments, likes এর জন্য ask করুন
  • Use Trending Sounds: Popular audio tracks ব্যবহার করুন
  • Post Consistently: Regular posting schedule maintain করুন

TikTok Content Types

বিভিন্ন ধরনের content format TikTok এ successful হয়।

Popular Content Categories:

  • Educational Content: Tips, tutorials, how-to videos
  • Entertainment: Comedy, skits, funny videos
  • Behind-the-Scenes: Business/personal life glimpses
  • Challenges: Trending challenges participate করা
  • Product Demos: Product showcasing ও reviews
  • Storytelling: Personal stories ও experiences

Content Creation Formula:

TikTok Content Formula:

1. Hook (0-3 seconds): Attention grabber
2. Context (3-10 seconds): What's happening
3. Content (10-25 seconds): Main message/entertainment
4. Call-to-Action (25-30 seconds): What to do next

Example:
"Wait, you've been doing this wrong!" (Hook)
"Here's the right way to..." (Context)
[Demonstration] (Content)
"Try this and let me know!" (CTA)

Video Content Creation

Video Production Guidelines:

1. Technical Requirements:
  • Resolution: 1080x1920 (9:16 aspect ratio)
  • Duration: 15-60 seconds optimal
  • File Size: Maximum 287MB
  • Frame Rate: 30fps recommended
2. Content Planning:
  1. Trending sounds research করুন
  2. Hook script লিখুন
  3. Visual elements plan করুন
  4. Call-to-action decide করুন
3. Shooting Tips:
  • Good Lighting: Natural light বা ring light ব্যবহার করুন
  • Stable Camera: Tripod বা steady hand
  • Clear Audio: Background noise minimize করুন
  • Eye Level: Camera eye level এ রাখুন
Sample TikTok Script Template:

Second 1-3: "Did you know this marketing trick?"
Second 4-8: "Most businesses miss this..."
Second 9-20: [Show the technique/tip]
Second 21-25: "Try this for your business!"
Second 26-30: "Follow for more tips! #marketing"
