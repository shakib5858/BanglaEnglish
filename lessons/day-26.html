Strategic Digital Marketing Planning

Strategic Planning ছাড়া digital marketing efforts fragmented হয়ে যায়। ৭১% successful companies strategic approach follow করে।

Strategic Planning এর সুবিধা
  • Clear Direction: Focused marketing efforts
  • Resource Optimization: Better budget allocation
  • Measurable Goals: Trackable objectives
  • Competitive Advantage: Market positioning
  • ROI Maximization: Efficient spending

Strategic Framework (SOSTAC):

Element Focus Key Questions Deliverable
Situation Current State Where are we now? SWOT Analysis
Objectives Goals Where do we want to be? SMART Goals
Strategy Approach How do we get there? Strategic Plan
Tactics Implementation What specific actions? Action Plan
Actions Execution Who does what when? Timeline
Control Measurement How do we monitor? KPI Dashboard

Omnichannel Marketing

Omnichannel Marketing সব touchpoints এ consistent customer experience তৈরি করে। Omnichannel companies ৯১% বেশি customer retention পায়।

Omnichannel vs Multichannel:

  • Multichannel: Multiple separate channels
  • Omnichannel: Integrated, seamless experience
  • Customer-centric: User journey focused
  • Data Integration: Unified customer view
  • Consistent Messaging: Brand coherence

Omnichannel Components:

  • Website: Central hub
  • Mobile App: On-the-go experience
  • Social Media: Community engagement
  • Email: Direct communication
  • Physical Store: Offline touchpoint
  • Customer Service: Support channels
  • Advertising: Paid touchpoints
Omnichannel Success Factors

Customer data integration, consistent branding, ও seamless handoffs সব channels এর মধ্যে।

Strategic Planning Workshop

SWOT Analysis Framework:

SWOT Analysis Template:

STRENGTHS (Internal Positive)
- Unique value proposition
- Strong brand recognition
- Skilled team
- Technology advantages
- Customer loyalty

WEAKNESSES (Internal Negative)
- Limited budget
- Lack of expertise
- Poor website performance
- Weak social presence
- Limited resources

OPPORTUNITIES (External Positive)
- Market growth
- New technologies
- Competitor weaknesses
- Regulatory changes
- Partnership possibilities

THREATS (External Negative)
- Economic downturn
- Increased competition
- Technology disruption
- Regulatory restrictions
- Market saturation

SMART Goals Setting:

  • Specific: Clear, well-defined objectives
  • Measurable: Quantifiable metrics
  • Achievable: Realistic targets
  • Relevant: Business-aligned goals
  • Time-bound: Deadline-driven
Example SMART Goals:
  • "Increase website organic traffic by 50% in 6 months"
  • "Generate 200 qualified leads per month through content marketing"
  • "Achieve 25% email open rate improvement in Q2"
  • "Reduce customer acquisition cost by 30% this year"
